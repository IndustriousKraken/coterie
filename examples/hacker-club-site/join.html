> man membership-faq

What do I get as a member?

Access to weekly meetings, workshops, our Discord server, and use of club equipment during events. Standard and Lifetime members can vote on club decisions.

How does payment work?

After signing up, you'll be able to pay through our secure member portal. We accept credit/debit cards via Stripe. Cash and check payments can be arranged at meetings.

Can I try before I join?

Absolutely! Visitors are welcome at regular meetings. Come check us out before committing.

Is there a refund policy?

Memberships are non-refundable, but we're confident you'll love being part of HEHC.
