Join Metro Baduk Club

Ready to join our community of Go enthusiasts? Fill out the form below to apply for membership. We welcome players of all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced dan players.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to all club nights and casual play sessions
  • Discounted entry to tournaments
  • Free teaching games with stronger players
  • Access to club equipment (boards, stones, clocks)
  • Invitations to special events and pro visits
  • Access to the member portal and online rankings
