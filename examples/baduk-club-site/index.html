About Our Club

The Metro Baduk Club has been bringing Go players together since 2018. Whether you call it Go, Baduk, or Weiqi, you'll find a welcoming community here dedicated to this beautiful game of strategy.

We welcome players of all levels. Our teaching program pairs beginners with experienced players for even games using handicap stones, while our advanced study groups analyze professional games and explore deep theory.
