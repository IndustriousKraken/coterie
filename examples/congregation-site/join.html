Get Connected

We're so glad you're interested in connecting with Unity Community Church! Whether you're looking to visit for the first time, become a member, or find ways to get involved, we're here to help.

Join Our Community

Create an account to stay connected with our church family. Members can access the directory, sign up for events, and stay informed about what's happening in our community.

Other Ways to Connect

Visit Us: The best way to get to know us is to join us for worship. Our services are Sunday at 9:00 AM and 10:30 AM. Look for our Welcome Center in the lobby where our hospitality team would love to meet you.

New Member Classes: Interested in learning more about our church? We offer monthly orientation sessions where you can learn about our history, beliefs, and the many ways to get involved.

Small Groups: We believe life is better in community. Our small groups meet throughout the week for Bible study, fellowship, and support.

Serve: From children's ministry to community outreach, there are countless ways to use your gifts to serve others. Talk to us about finding your fit.
