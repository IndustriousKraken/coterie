Welcome Home

At Unity Community Church, we believe that everyone has a place at the table. Our doors are open to seekers and believers, to those with questions and those with answers, to the young and the young at heart.

We gather each week for worship, fellowship, and learning. Our services blend traditional hymns with contemporary music, thoughtful preaching with quiet reflection, and ancient wisdom with modern relevance.

Beyond Sunday mornings, we offer small groups for every interest, service opportunities in our community, and programs for all ages. There's a place for you here.
