Welcome to the Hipster Electronics Hacking Club, where we celebrate the golden age of personal computing. We're a community of enthusiasts who appreciate the elegance of 8-bit processors, the satisfying click of mechanical keyboards, and the warm glow of phosphor displays.

Whether you're restoring a TRS-80, programming a Commodore 64, or building something new with a vintage aesthetic, you'll find your people here.
